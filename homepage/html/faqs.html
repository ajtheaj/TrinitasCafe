FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Using your queue number, you may track the progress of your order by going to the stall. The waiting time is approximately 5-10 minutes.
You can place an order by selecting items from the menu and proceeding to the checkout page. Choose your payment method, and you're all set.
We accept various payment methods such as GCash, Bank Transfer, and Cash on Delivery.
